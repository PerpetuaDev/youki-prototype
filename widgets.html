Widgets — final deliverables

Two widget behaviours: a Dawn forecast widget that always shows tomorrow morning's predicted colours and counts down to golden hour, and a Live sky widget that simulates whatever the sky looks like right now and tracks the next event through the whole day — same event-adjusted logic as the app's home stack. Plus a minimal Colour bar variant and the monochrome lock-screen pieces.
